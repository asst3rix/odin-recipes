Vinaigrette

Description

Vinaigrette is like the life of the salad party—smooth, tangy, and always a little zesty! It’s a sassy mix of vinegar and olive oil, shaken up with a pinch of salt, a crack of pepper, and maybe a dab of Dijon to keep things interesting.

This dressing isn’t just for salads; it’s a flavor bomb that’s here to wake up your veggies and give them a little attitude. Customize it with a drizzle of honey, some fresh herbs, or even a splash of citrus, and you’ve got yourself a dressing that’s ready to steal the show!

Ingredients

  • 20g of Dijon mustard
  • 10g of Dijon wholegrain mustard
  • 5cl of balsmic vinegar
  • 15cl of oil (olive or sunflower)
  • Sesame oil
  • Salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Put the Dijon mustard, Dijon wholegrain mustard, balsamic vinegar, salt and pepper in a bowl and stir it.
  2. Progressively add the oil (olive or sunflower) while stirring.
  3. Add a few drops of sesame oil and stir. Taste it and adjust.
  4. Put it in the fridge for 30 minutes and enjoy!
