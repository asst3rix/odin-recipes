Mayonnaise

Description

Mayonnaise is the creamy, dreamy spread that’s always up for making things tastier! With just a smooth blend of egg yolks, oil, and a dash of vinegar or lemon juice, mayo is like the secret sauce that brings sandwiches, salads, and dips to the next level.

It’s thick, luscious, and perfectly rich, ready to add that extra oomph to whatever you’re making. Want to jazz it up? Add garlic, herbs, or a touch of spice, and watch mayo go from classic to totally crave-worthy!

Ingredients

  • 1 table spoon of Dijon mustard
  • 2 table spoons of lemon juice
  • 1 egg
  • 1 glass of sunflower oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Put everything in a mixer and mix it until good texture.
  2. Put it in the fridge for 30 minutes and enjoy it!
