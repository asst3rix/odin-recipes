Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the ultimate pasta party in a baking dish! Imagine layers of tender pasta sheets hugging a saucy blend of juicy tomatoes, savory ground meat, and creamy ricotta, all crowned with a golden layer of bubbling mozzarella or parmesan cheese.

Each bite is a deliciously cozy stack of flavor—melty, cheesy, and just the right amount of saucy! Whether it’s a traditional meaty version or a veggie-packed twist, lasagna is the go-to comfort food that always brings people to the table with a smile.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Tomato sauce
  • Béchamel
  • Parmigiano
  • Basilic

Steps

  1. Turn on the oven and put it at 220°.
  2. Cut the onion and put it in the pan with a bit of olive oil.
  3. After a few minutes add the meat, let it cook a bit and then add the tomato sauce.
  4. Prepare the béchamel on the side.
  5. When everything is ready, you can start by putting the first layer of pasta in your dish. Then put the meat, béchamel and parmigiano.
  6. Repeat this as much as you want but always finish by a layer of pasta with béchamel and parmigiano.
  7. Put the dish in the oven for approximately 30 minutes.
  8. Put a bit of fresh basilic on the top and it's ready to be enjoyed!
